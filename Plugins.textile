h2. Leiningen Plugins

* "lein-swank":http://github.com/technomancy/swank-clojure Launch a Swank server for Emacs integration. (now included in swank-clojure)
* "lein-release":http://github.com/technomancy/lein-release Create a tarball of your project and its dependencies
* "lein-play":http://github.com/technomancy/lein-play Play a different sound at the end of your test runs depending on whether they pass or fail.
